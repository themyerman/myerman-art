There Are Two Wolves Inside You

You've seen this story attributed to approximately every Indigenous nation on the continent. The actual origin is disputed. But the idea — that we are in constant negotiation with our worst and best selves — is old enough that it doesn't need a verified source. I made this piece because the story is true even if the attribution is messy.
