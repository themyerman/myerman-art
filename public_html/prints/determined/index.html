Determined

I'm here because my ancestors refused to yield. Through genocide, ecocide, ethnocide — they held on. Not metaphorically. Literally. They were determined to survive in a way most people will never have to be. This piece is for them. And it's a reminder to me, every day, that persistence is not passive.
