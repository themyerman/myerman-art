Moonlight Fishing

I was thinking about our long ago woodland Indian ancestors when I painted this. Thinking about how they got around in birchbark canoes. And I'd always seen those heroic paintings of a war party striking out in canoes, looking to mix it up. But what about the quieter moments? Fishing at night?
